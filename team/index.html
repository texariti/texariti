About Us

We are a team of web designers and programmers who are joining together to build state-of-the-art web projects.

TeXariti sounds like the words ‘tech’ and ‘care’ with an ‘iti’ on the end. Xariti sounds like ‘charity’ but with a ‘K’.

What is “TeXariti”?

TeXariti comes from two Greek words and means a gift that is not earned. Our name was chosen to remind us that we didn't get where we are alone. We want to use our gifts to help others.
