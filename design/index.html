Is your
website
boring?

When phones moved from being dumb to smart, the importance having a good website skyrocketed. Most people now check out a company or church online before going. Think of it... the majority of the people who walk through your doors look at your website before come.

When people visit your website, what do they see?

  • What message does it send?
  • Does it make them want to spend some time on your site?
  • Does it catch their attention?
  • Does it make them want your product or service?
  • Do they think it is creative?
  • Does it make them want to tell others to take a look at your site?
  • Does it make them want to come?

The emotional impact of your website is the key to the success of your business and the message you wish to convey.

What do you want to accomplish with your website? Is the website you have fulfilling this purpose? If not, you could be missing out on a lot of business.

Art-Directed Design

The way to have a great website is pretty simple: Put the content first. Tell your story. Tell the stories of the people who believe in you. And have everything on your website revolve around that.

We are a new web design agency, TeXariti, and we specialize in what is called art-directed design. Art-directed design puts your message first, and the entire design of a page exists to promote that message.

Each major page of an art-designed website has its own look, its own colors, its own character. And everything is used to the effect of pointing at the message. The result can be stunning!

You have complete creative control of your website. When you think of something creative, go for it!

Mobile-Ready

The latest statistics say that 70% of all internet viewing is now by mobile phone or tablet. Most websites, those created two or more years ago, are not mobile-ready. I use a framework that automatically adapts to look good on all mobile devices. Your website will look good on a big monitor, a small monitor, a tablet or phone.

You might tell people about your website at meetings or tradeshows. You want them to be able to pull up your website on their phone, and it needs to look good and work well. Not all websites can do that.
